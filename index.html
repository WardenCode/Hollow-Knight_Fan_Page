Hollow Knight

In wilds beyond they speak your name with reverence and regret, For none could tame our savage souls yet you the challenge met, Under palest watch, you taught, we changed, base instincts were redeemed, A world you gave to bug and beast as they had never dreamed.

- From 'Elegy for Hallownest' by Monomon the Teacher

Hollow Kight Knight is a indie game of the metroidvania genre created by Team Cherry.

In the game we play a knight who arrives at the Hallownest Kingdom, where we will find many dangers and creatures that attack us for no apparent reason, the further we enter the kingdom the more we will discover its secrets and the purpose of our knight. Throughout the adventure we will find multiple characters with unique personalities of which you will know more about the lore of the game.

Characters

The Knight

A vessel, knight born of darkness a hero with a duty to "Fight with the 'light'". And end his control over the kingdom.

Hornet

Hornet is the mysterious princess protector of Hallownest. She handles a needle and thread with deadly dexterity. Raised on Deepnest, Hornet survived the Infection and the fall of the kingdom.

Quirrel

Former apprentice to the dreamer Monomon. He was entrusted with the mission of preserving a mask and leaving the kingdom losing his memory. This insect that roams Hallownest in wonder, Quirrel was drawn to this place without knowing why.

The Radiance

The Radiance is a higher being of light similar to the Essence and, as such, opposed to the Void, its ancient enemy. The Moth Tribe is born from his light and, in return, worships it.

Pale King

God who used to be the monarch of Hallownest. He previously he was a Wyrm moving through the mountains and we stopped until he finally stopped at "The Edge of the Realm". He left his shell and became what we know as "Pale King" since he was reduced in size obtaining the appearance of a Hallownest insect.
